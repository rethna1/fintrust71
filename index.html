KYC Process — Step by Step (New Investors)

Follow these simple steps to complete your KYC and start investing. Most investors complete Aadhaar OTP–based e-KYC in just a few minutes.

  1. 1
    Prepare Required Documents
    Keep your PAN card, Aadhaar number, mobile linked to Aadhaar, email ID, and bank account details (cancelled cheque or passbook).
  2. 2
    Start Online KYC
    Begin Aadhaar OTP–based e-KYC (fastest) or complete digital KYC by uploading PAN and Aadhaar images if Aadhaar OTP is not available. Some platforms may guide you to Video KYC for verification.
  3. 3
    Fill Personal Information
    Enter your name, date of birth, address, mobile number, and email exactly as per PAN and Aadhaar records to avoid rejection.
  4. 4
    Complete Aadhaar OTP / Video KYC
    Verify your identity using Aadhaar OTP. If Aadhaar OTP is not accessible, the platform may request Video KYC for face and document verification.
  5. 5
    Add Bank Details
    Enter your bank account number, IFSC, and upload a cancelled cheque if required. A penny-drop verification will confirm account ownership.
  6. 6
    KYC Approval
    Most Aadhaar OTP–based KYCs are approved instantly. Digital or Video KYC approvals may take a few hours. After approval, you can start SIP, Lumpsum, or SWP immediately.
Disclaimer: The KYC process and required documents may vary depending on the AMC, registrar (KFintech / CAMS), or investment platform. This guide is for general investor assistance. Please follow the instructions shown on the respective platform during onboarding.
